## Description of Service

TOTP Authenticator is a two-factor authentication (2FA) app that generates Time-based One-Time Passwords (TOTP) according to RFC 6238. The App provides:

- Generation of 6 or 8 digit TOTP codes
- Support for SHA1, SHA256, and SHA512 algorithms
- QR code scanning for easy account setup
- iCloud sync for account metadata
- Apple Watch companion app
- Home Screen and Lock Screen widgets
- AutoFill integration for iOS
